AI Digest — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

AI Digest (the “App”) is a daily briefing on artificial intelligence. It publishes a summarized news digest that you can read without an account. This policy explains what data the App handles and why.

Who we are

AI Digest is operated by Nitai Agarwal. For any privacy question or request, contact nit.agar88@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Nothing is required to read the digest. You can browse the daily briefing anonymously; no account or personal data is needed.
  • If you sign in with Google (optional, used only to sync your saved stories across devices), we receive and store your Google account email address and a user identifier. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Bookmarks. Stories you save are stored so they sync to your account. If you are not signed in, bookmarks stay only on your device.
  • Read history (which stories you have opened) is stored locally on your device and is not sent to us.
  • Push notifications. If you allow notifications, the App may register with Google Firebase Cloud Messaging to alert you when a new digest is published.
  • Diagnostics. If crash reporting is enabled, Google Firebase may collect anonymous crash and diagnostic data to help us fix bugs.

We do not collect your contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers for advertising, and the App contains no advertising or third-party tracking SDKs.

How your data is used

The limited data above is used solely to: keep you signed in, sync your saved stories, deliver the notifications you opted into, and diagnose crashes. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising or profiling.

Where your data is stored

Account and bookmark data is stored using Supabase (a hosted PostgreSQL service) on servers located in Australia. Access is restricted by row-level security so that only you can read or modify your own account and bookmarks.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (see their privacy policy).
  • Google Sign-In & Firebase — optional authentication and push notifications (see the Google privacy policy).

News summaries are generated on our server from publicly available articles; this process does not involve your personal data.

Data retention and deletion

Your account and bookmarks are kept until you ask us to delete them. To delete your account and all associated data, email nit.agar88@gmail.com from the address you signed in with, and we will remove it. Signing out and uninstalling the App also clears the data stored on your device.

Children

AI Digest is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
