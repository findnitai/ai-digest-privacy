Delete Your AI Digest Account & Data

AI Digest (the “App”) lets you read the daily briefing without an account. If you signed in with Google to sync your saved stories, you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

Email nit.agar88@gmail.com from the Google account email address you used to sign in, with the subject “Delete my AI Digest account”.

We verify the request comes from your sign-in address and delete your account and data within 30 days, then confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Your account record and the Google email address associated with it.
  • All of your synced bookmarks (saved stories).

What is kept

Nothing personal is retained after deletion. The App's news digests are public content generated from published articles and are not tied to your account. Data stored only on your device (read history, and any bookmarks made while signed out) is removed when you sign out or uninstall the App.
